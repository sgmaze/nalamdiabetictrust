Meet the Founders

We are a dedicated group of students from the USA with deep family roots in Coimbatore, committed to bridging the healthcare gap through technology, engineering, and community service.

PS

High School Student

Pranav focuses on the operational and strategic goals of the foundation, ensuring that outreach is effectively reaching the villages that need it most.

AD
